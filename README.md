# 🎵 Melaos Studios

> *Where Sound Meets Soul — an independent music studio and label built for artists who mean it.*

**Melaos Studios** is a next-generation music studio, label, and creative hub dedicated to cultivating raw talent, producing world-class sound, and building a movement rooted in artistry, culture, and authenticity.

---

## 🎙️ What We Do

| Service | Description |
|---|---|
| 🎤 **Recording & Production** | Professional studio sessions and full music production from concept to master |
| 🎚️ **Mixing & Mastering** | Clean, polished, radio-ready and streaming-ready sound |
| 🌟 **Artist Development** | A&R, coaching, branding, image strategy, and career planning |
| 🌐 **Digital Distribution** | Getting your music on every major platform — Spotify, Apple Music, TIDAL, and more |
| 🤝 **Collaborations** | Connecting artists, producers, and songwriters across genres and markets |
| 🎶 **Label Releases** | In-house projects, co-releases, and original catalog |

---

## 🌍 The Sound

Melaos Studios sits at the intersection of genres, cultures, and generations. We don't lock artists into a box — we find the sound that's already inside them and build it into something the world can feel.

| Genre DNA | Influence |
|---|---|
| Hip-Hop & R&B | East Coast grit, Southern soul |
| Afrobeats & Afro-Fusion | Lagos energy, global reach |
| Pop & Electronic | Crossover appeal, streaming-first production |
| Soul & Neo-Soul | Timeless feel, deep roots |
| Drill & Trap | Raw, authentic, street-born |

## 🤝 Contributing

Melaos Studios is a growing ecosystem. If you're an artist, producer, engineer, or creative looking to contribute:

1. Email us at **cryptofedge@gmail.com**
2. Drop your portfolio / demo links
3. We'll review and get back within 72 hours

For technical contributions to this repo:
1. Fork the repo
2. Create a feature branch (`git checkout -b feature/your-idea`)
3. Commit your changes (`git commit -m 'Add: your feature'`)
4. Push to the branch (`git push origin feature/your-idea`)
5. Open a Pull Request
