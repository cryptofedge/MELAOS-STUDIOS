## About

**Melaos Studios** is an independent music studio and label dedicated to cultivating raw talent, producing world-class sound, and building a movement rooted in artistry, culture, and authenticity.

We blend cutting-edge production with timeless musicality — creating a space where artists grow, thrive, and leave their mark on the world.

---

## What We Do

- 🎙️ **Recording & Production** — Professional-grade studio sessions and full music production
- 🎚️ **Mixing & Mastering** — Clean, polished, radio-ready sound
- 🎤 **Artist Development** — A&R, coaching, branding, and career strategy for emerging artists
- 🌐 **Digital Distribution** — Getting your music heard on every major platform
- 🎶 **Original Catalog** — In-house projects, collabs, and label releases
